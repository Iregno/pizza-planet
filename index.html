Our Story

Pizza Planet was founded in 2010 by two best friends who shared a passion for great pizza and space!Our mission is to serve the most delicous pizza in the galaxy while providing a fun expereience for the whole family.
All our ingredients are fresh and locally sourced.Our dough is made in-house daily,and our source is a secret family recipe passed down through generations,
